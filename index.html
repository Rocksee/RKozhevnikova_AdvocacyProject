Mission

At Lucky Paws Animal Rescue, our mission is to provide a safe haven for homeless cats and dogs, offering compassionate care, fostering opportunities, and the chance for every animal to find a loving, forever home. We are dedicated to promoting responsible pet ownership, supporting our community, and reducing shelter overcrowding through rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption.
